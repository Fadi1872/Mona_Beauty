About us

More than just makeup, Mona Beauty is a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. We believe beauty starts from within, and our mission is to provide you with the tools and inspiration to unlock your natural radiance.

Mona Beauty wasn't planned, it was discovered! Dr. Mona Alrayes, trekking the Amazon, stumbled upon a hidden tribe known for their radiant skin. Intrigued by their ancient rituals and rare botanicals, she returned home inspired to share these beauty secrets. Thus, Mona Beauty was born, bridging the gap between ancient wisdom and modern science.
